    Description of Game

    Tic Tac Toe, noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. It involves looking ahead and trying to figure out what the person playing against you might do next.


    Rules of the Game

    1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.

    2. Let you are X, your friend (or the computer) is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.

    3. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.

    4. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
